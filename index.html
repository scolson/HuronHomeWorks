Company Policies

We want every project to go smoothly and feel stress-free. These simple policies help keep scheduling fair, communication clear, and expectations aligned for everyone

Scheduling

  • We schedule projects on a first-come, first-served basis after reviewing your project details
  • You’ll receive an appointment window to allow for travel, weather, and typical job variability (especially in Michigan!)
  • An adult (18+) must be present during the appointment

Estimate

  • All estimates are valid for 10 days from the date issued
  • Prices are based on visible and known conditions at the time of the estimate
  • If hidden or unforeseen issues are discovered (for example, things inside walls), we’ll discuss options and provide an updated estimate or change order before moving forward

Payment

  • Payment is due at the completion of the job unless stated otherwise
  • We accept cash, and common digital payment methods
  • Materials required require prepayment to reserve your time on the schedule
  • Late invoices will incur a fee to cover administrative costs

Cancellation & Rescheduling

  • 48 hours notice is required for cancellations
  • If less than 48 hours notice is given, the deposit will be forfeited to cover lost time and scheduling costs
  • Rescheduling due to a documented emergency may be allowed on a case-by-case basis. A small fee may apply to cover the impact to our schedule
  • If we arrive and cannot access the property (no adult home, locked out, pets not secured, unsafe conditions), the trip fee will be forfeited

Scope & Licensing

  • We handle a wide range of handyman and home repair tasks
  • We do not perform work requiring licensed trades (e.g., major electrical, plumbing, or HVAC) unless stated
  • If your project needs a specialist or a permit, we’ll let you know right away and can refer trusted local pros

Safety

  • Please make sure the work area is clean, accessible, and safe before our arrival
  • Pets must be secured for everyone’s safety
  • We may decline any task that is unsafe or beyond the reasonable duties of a handyman
  • Please let us know in advance about any known hazards—water leaks, electrical issues, pests, mold, etc

Clean-Up

  • Basic cleanup is included with every job: sweeping, removing job-related debris, and leaving the area tidy
  • Disposal of large items, old appliances, old construction materials, or dump runs may require an additional fee

Change Order

  • If you decide to add work or change materials mid-project, no problem—we’re flexible!
  • We’ll provide a quick updated price or change order so you know exactly what to expect before we continue

Warranty & Quality

  • All workmanship is covered by a 30-day quality guarantee
  • Manufacturer warranties apply to items we purchase and install when available
  • Issues caused by misuse, owner-supplied materials, or changes to the home after installation aren’t covered, but we’ll always help find a solution
  • Before and after photos and videos are taken to document the project for proof of completion as well as marketing purposes.
